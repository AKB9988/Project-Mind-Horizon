NEET - National Eligibility cum Entrance Test


The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate medical and dental courses in India. NEET is the sole exam for admission to MBBS, BDS, and other health-related programs in government and private institutions. It tests candidates on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology based on the NCERT curriculum for classes 11 and 12.

NEET Exam 2025 Overview

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level entrance exam in India for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate medical and dental courses. NEET serves as the gateway to MBBS, BDS, and other health-related programs in prestigious medical institutions across the country. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), this exam ensures a fair and transparent selection process for candidates seeking admission to medical colleges.

Types of NEET Exam

The NEET exam primarily consists of a single paper that assesses the knowledge of students in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. The exam follows a pen-and-paper format with multiple-choice questions.

NEET 2025 Exam Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet specific educational and age-related criteria to appear for NEET 2025. Below is a summary of the NEET 2025 eligibility conditions.

NEET 2025 Exam Eligibility Criteria
Criteria Details
Educational Qualification Must have completed Class 12 or an equivalent examination with subjects: Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Biotechnology.
Year of Passing Candidates who passed Class 12 in 2023, appearing in 2024, or will appear in 2025 are eligible.
Subjects Required Physics, Chemistry, and Biology are mandatory subjects.
Minimum Marks General category: Minimum 50% marks in Class 12. For SC/ST/OBC, minimum 40% marks. For PwD candidates, minimum 45% marks.
Age Limit Minimum age: 17 years as of December 31, 2025.
Number of Attempts No restriction on the number of attempts.

NEET 2025 Exam Pattern

NEET 2025 will follow a similar pattern to previous years, with one single paper consisting of 180 questions divided into three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

NEET 2025 Exam Pattern Overview
Particulars Details
Mode of Exam Pen-and-paper based (Offline)
Duration 3 hours
Number of Questions 180 (Physics: 45, Chemistry: 45, Biology: 90)
Total Marks 720
Type of Questions Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Marking Scheme +4 for correct answers, -1 for incorrect answers, 0 for unattempted questions
Languages 13 languages available (English, Hindi, regional languages)
